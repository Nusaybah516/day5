About

Restaurant des Amoureux is one of the major tourist sites in Paris,France.It is known for it's sweet desserts and it's couple events.
Our Restaurant was established in 1956 by Monsieur Jon Bruce as a gift to his wife Madame Rakel who loved making desserts.
We open 8am-6pm on weekdays and 9am-8pm on weekends.We organise karoke night every wednesday.Couple's night is held the second and last Saturdays of the month.
